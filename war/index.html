Welcome

We provide an open forum for discussion for members of LUU and the Leeds Community, promoting critical thinking, open discussion, free speech and uncensored debate. Training is available and opportunities for debating and public speaking. LDU have a national and international reputation for excellence.

Our Reputation

Our members have travelled across the world from europe to south-east asia to deliever debating training and to take part in internation competitions. We have a history of success at many national and international competitions with our best speakers reaching finals and semi-finals of large prestigous competitions.

Our Training

Debating, as a discipline, gives you skills that your degree demands yet cannot focus on. Our members are taught to argument construction, deconstruction, structure and analysis. Without expection, our members have improved their seminar contributions and essay marks due to our training.

Our Meetings

We meet every thursday and tuesday, where we train members in workshops and seminars then run a debate. Debates are judged by our senior members with years of professional training and speaking experience.

View details »

How

Debating asks our members to engage with the varied facets public speaking: clarity of expression, phonology, tempo, rhetoric and argumentation. Skills our members find invaluable in later life. We teach diverse topics from the ethics of euthanasia to humanitarian intervention, emphasising the presentation of meaningful content in an engaging way.
