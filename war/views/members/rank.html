Selection Ranking

Input Information

Tab Median is the number of speaker points you get 50% of the time.

Trial Average is the average number of speaker points you get at the trial.

Consistency is how much you vary as a speaker. If you vary about two speaks from your average throughout a day, you have a consistency of 80; if you vary 3 speaks you have 70 and so on..

Service services performed on behalf of the society.

Reputation the extent to which you will enhance the reputation of LDU: if you are offensive and arrogant you get a low rep, if you are kind and polite you get a high rep.

Output Information

Rank is out of 100; rank is shifted so that all 75 inputs produce a rank of 75. The rank is very compact between "usual" input ranges, so that speakers may find themselves differentiated by one point or less.

Bracket: Speakers are aggressively grouped in brackets of 0.5 points for the purposes of funding and differentiation. Bracket indicates your likely bracket, indicated by the number of 0.5 steps from 70. Real brackets are calculated from the number of steps above the lowest rank recorded.

Merit is the contribution from your Median/Average/Consistency

Goodness is the contribution from your Service/Reputation
